Privacy Policy

For Application: TAM

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TAM, a productivity RPG developed by SATER Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy while you embark on your hero's journey.

2. Local-First Data Philosophy

TAM operates on a local-first basis.

  • No PII Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit Personal Identifiable Information (PII) to external servers.
  • Local Storage: Your tasks, XP, levels, and RPG stats (FOC, SOL, NRG) are stored exclusively on your device's local database.

3. Permissions & Their Purpose

To provide the full RPG experience, TAM requires the following permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO (Soul Echoes / Voice Vault): Used only to allow you to record voice notes. These recordings stay on your device and are never uploaded.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to display the "Golden Icon" (Crown) in your status bar during focus sessions and to notify you of victories.
  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback (Double-Pulse) to celebrate task completion and session ends.

4. PDF Export & Storage Access

TAM requests storage access for the sole purpose of:

  • SaterPdfExporter: Allowing you to generate and save productivity reports as PDF files directly to your device.

5. Third-Party Libraries

We use standard, trusted Flutter libraries (such as flutter_local_notifications and audioplayers).

  • No Analytics/Ads: This initial version of TAM does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

6. Children’s Privacy

TAM is a tool for productivity. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Contact Information

For any inquiries regarding your privacy or the SATER_ ecosystem, contact us at: beatnabytna@gmail.com
